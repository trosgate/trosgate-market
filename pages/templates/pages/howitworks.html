The Manager

When it comes to freelancers, nothing can be achieved without a team. Every freelancer, including external invites are assigned a default Team. If there is a team, there is a manager/founder.

At {{website.site_name}}, the creator of a team is called the manager or founder. As such, the manager assumes the task of receiving all email notifications and payments on behalf of the team.
